About Us

Hyperflex Labs is an investment institution focusing on Web3 industry.
Its investment portfolio consists of three categories: blockchain infrastructure construction, early-stage blockchain projects investment, and open financial Ecosystem services, with the mission of integrating traditional capital and infrastructure resources into the field of blockchain and accelerating cross-field collaboration.

Digital Fund

About Us

Hyperflex Labs is a premier investment and incubation platform dedicated to advancing and scaling blockchain innovations. We provide comprehensive support through venture funding, technology solutions, community management, legal and compliance advisory, and a wide array of tailored resources.
By incubating and investing in high-potential projects at an early stage, Infinity Labs Digital Fund has built a diversified and forward-looking portfolio that includes notable names such as Crust, TON Foundation, Dora Factory, and Matters. This strategic approach enables us to deploy capital across key verticals within the blockchain ecosystem, capturing exceptional value in the industry’s nascent phases.

Veteran Team

About Us

Hyperflex Labs is powered by a veteran team with deep-rooted expertise across the entire Web3 spectrum. Our members hail from diverse yet complementary backgrounds—traditional finance, law, technology, marketing, and product management—bringing a wealth of experience to the table.
